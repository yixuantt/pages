About FinEmbed

FinEmbed is a financial text embedding service that provides pre-trained models for financial text analysis. Our models are trained on a large corpus of financial news articles, earnings call transcripts, and SEC filings to generate high-quality embeddings for financial text data.

Our Mission

We are a team dedicated to building the best financial text embedding models to empower quantitative traders and financial service applications.
